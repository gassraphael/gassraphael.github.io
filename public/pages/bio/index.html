Honorary distinctions

  • 2023: Guo Konghui award, as best young author, at the 7th CAA International Conference on Vehicular Control and Intelligence, for the paper entitled A 1-D 2-phase control-oriented mass transfer model of PEM fuel cells (Changsha, China)

Professional Experience

  • 2025 - 2027: Postdoctoral Researcher in PEM fuel cell simulation at University of Reunion island at the Energy-Lab.

    • Partnership: ZSW Institute in Ulm, Germany.
    • Project: Optimization of the performance and durability of hydrogen systems using an advanced digital twin (OPUS-H2).
    • Funding: European FEDER and national counterparts.

  • 2021 - 2024: PhD in control engineering at Aix-Marseille University at the LIS Laboratory.

    • Co-supervision: Franche-Comté University at the FEMTO-ST Institute within FCLab.
    • Project: Durability enhancement of fuel cell electric vehicles by exploring multi-level learning-based control (DEAL).

  • 2021 - 2024: Part-time lecturer in Mathematics and Computer Science at Aix-Marseille University and Franche-Comté University.

  • 2020 - 2021: Research assistant intern at the Center for Materials Science and Structures (SMS) at Mines Saint-Etienne.

    • Project: Development of a calculation code for hydrogen diffusion in a porous metal. Development of an experimental protocol for measuring hydrogen desorption.

  • 2020: Assistant engineer intern at EDF Hydraulique, France.

    • Project: Assistance in the digitalization of maintenance for hydraulic plants.

  • 2019: Worker intern at CIMC Air Marrel, France.

    • Project: Assembly of parts for the creation of loaders destined for airports.

Graduate schools

  • 2021-2024: PhD in control engineering at Aix-Marseille University in LIS laboratory, and at Franche-Comté University at FEMTO-ST institute within the FCLab.

    • Thesis subject: Advanced physical modeling of PEM fuel cells to enhance their performances.
    • The defense report and the reports on the thesis manuscript are available here.

  • 2018-2021: Master degree in general engineering, with a double master degree in process engineering at Mines Saint-Etienne, from Mines-Telecom Institute (Saint-Étienne, France).

    • The certification of the diploma can be obtained through the Mines Saint-Etienne Alumni webpage.
    • Europass, an institution of the European Union, gives informations on these diplomas which are specific to the French education system.

  • 2015-2018: Higher school preparatory classes at Leconte De Lisle (Reunion island, France) and La Martinière Monplaisir (Lyon, France).

    • This institution, specific to the French system, corresponds to the preferred path for obtaining admission to a French top graduate school.
